2-Step Account Verification

BiobankCloud ensures strong security to protect your data. We provide you secure access to the BiobankCloud through 2-step verification:

  • In step 1, you enter your usual password


  • In step 2, you enter a one-time PIN code that is generated by the Goolge Authenticator app in your device

You are required to have a smartphone and install the Google Authenticator app. More guidelines about setting up a 2-step verification can be followed at Google Authenticator.

  • Download the Authenticator app in Google Play and install it in your phone
  • Open the Authenticator app and then select the "Scan account barcode" (label 1)
  • If you don't have a barcode scanner app, then install the barcode scanner (label 2)
  • After installation, reopen Authenticator and point it to your camera at the QR code on your computer screen. This is where you have submitted the registration form successfully
  • Download the Authenticator app in the on the Windows Phone Store.
  • Open the Authenticator app and tap the add (+) button to set up the account
  • Then point the camera to the barcode and press scan
  • Download the Authenticator app in the App store and install it in your phone
  • Open the Authenticator app
  • Click on the "Scan Barcode" and then point your camera at the QR code on your computer screen. This is where you have submitted the registration form successfully

In 2-step verification, there is a little risk your account becomes compromised and put your sensitive data at risk. For example, bad guys are not able to steal your passwords anymore and misuse your data in the BiobankCloud.



We care about security and privacy of your data, and that is why we require you to use 2-step verification.
